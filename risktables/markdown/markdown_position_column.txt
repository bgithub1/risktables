### position column help:
 * position colum values can be any positive or negative number
 * dollar value of position = price * position
   * negative numbers are short positions
   * 10 IBM trading @ $190 = $1900
 * Commodities position sizes **ARE NOT CONTRACTS**
   * -10 CLQ19 trading @ $36.25 = -$362.50 
     * **(NOT -$362,500.00)**
   * 10 ZSU19 trading @ 925 = $9,250 
     * **(NOT 9.25 * 5000 = $46,250.00)**
